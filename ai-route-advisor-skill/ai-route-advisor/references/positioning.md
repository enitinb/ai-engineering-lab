# Positioning

AI Route Advisor is a vendor-neutral Claude Skill powered by R.E.S.E.T.

Tagline: Choose the right AI route before you choose the model.

Core promise: classify the work, assess risk, recommend a generic route type, define guardrails, and specify what must be evaluated before production.

Do not recommend named vendors, products, or specific models unless the user provides an approved catalog and explicitly asks for model-level mapping.
